# Contract Value Limits

Only one player option max per contract

## Rookie contract

Does it matter? Calculated based on draft position. Rules for frp and srps are different.

## Max Contract (not actually the max)

The amount of the cap you can receive is dependent on how long you've been in the NBA:

0-6: 25%
7-9: 30%
10+: 35%

The most that the contract can expand per year is 5%

The maximum number of years is 4


## Supermax

If you've been appointed the honors of all-NBA, DPOY, MVP, or all-NBA the last two seasons, you're eligible for the supermax:

35% of the cap, max 5 years.

There can only be 3 supermax players on the same team.
